# System Design Evaluation Criteria

Override base scoring with design assessment:

1. **Requirements analysis** (0-10): Did they clarify scope and constraints?
2. **Architecture clarity** (0-10): Is the high-level design sound and well-structured?
3. **Depth of detail** (0-10): Can they deep-dive into specific components?
4. **Trade-off reasoning** (0-10): Do they articulate why they chose one approach over another?

Additional signals:
- Scalability thinking: Do they consider growth and bottlenecks?
- Reliability awareness: Do they discuss failure modes and recovery?
