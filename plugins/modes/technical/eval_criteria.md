# Technical Evaluation Criteria

Override base scoring with technical assessment:

1. **Correctness** (0-10): Is the technical answer accurate?
2. **Depth** (0-10): Does the answer go beyond surface-level knowledge?
3. **Breadth** (0-10): Can the candidate connect to related concepts?
4. **Practical application** (0-10): Can they apply theory to real scenarios?

Additional signals:
- Trade-off awareness: Can they discuss pros/cons of approaches?
- Edge case thinking: Do they consider boundary conditions?
