# HR Evaluation Criteria

Override base scoring with HR assessment:

1. **Career clarity** (0-10): Does the candidate have clear, realistic career goals?
2. **Culture alignment** (0-10): Do their values match typical company expectations?
3. **Communication quality** (0-10): Are they articulate and professional?
4. **Self-awareness** (0-10): Do they show honest self-reflection?

Additional signals:
- Red flags: Excessive negativity about past employers, unclear motivations
- Green flags: Growth mindset, specific examples, genuine enthusiasm
