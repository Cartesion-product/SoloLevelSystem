# Behavioral Evaluation Criteria

Override base scoring rubric with STAR-specific assessment:

1. **Situation clarity** (0-10): Did the candidate describe a clear, specific situation?
2. **Task ownership** (0-10): Did they explain THEIR role and responsibilities?
3. **Action detail** (0-10): Were actions specific, not generic platitudes?
4. **Result impact** (0-10): Were outcomes measurable and meaningful?

Additional signals:
- Self-awareness: Can they articulate lessons learned?
- Growth mindset: Do they show improvement from past experiences?
